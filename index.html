Top 5 tips on solo travelling
Choose your destination with intent
- You can travel better simply by understanding why you want to go where you want to go. Understand your intent, what’s drawing you to the destination, and you’ll have a more enriching travel experience.
Keep your accomodation to yourself
- Your accommodation is your safe haven. Don’t tell people where you’re staying. If they ask, it’s easy to be vague.
Meet other women travellers
-You can build friendships with women around the world. Our favorite way is by staying at hostels.
Protect your valuables
-Keep your passport and other important documents secure. Have backup copies on you and at home with your trusted contact.
Pack light
-You’re going to save money and be more mobile if you pack light.
